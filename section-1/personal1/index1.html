LIFE LESSONS LEARNT WHILE LEARNING TO CODE

Follow your own learning path because it is the only way to simplify your journey to becoming the best programmer you can be. Following a path laid out by someone else makes the journey unnecessarily difficult. The key(magic) is to take whatever resource you have access to and create your own learning path. In other words; master the concepts your way in a self-paced manner. As you faithfully do this you will gradually realize that your unique thinking patterns, thoughts, and your creative spirit will lead you to your goal far easier than by following someone else's path.

In line with the first point. Don't depend solely on others doing things for you. Try your best to do things for yourself. Do all in your power to get things done for yourself. When you allow others to do things for you or solve problems for you it's their solution and not yours. Consequently it becomes harder for you to internalize that solution and make it your own. When you think of or come up with a solution to a problem by yourself it makes you depend less on others to get problems solved. This way of life helps you increase your power in this world. As a bonus, when you are comfortable sovling problems yourself you easily pick up tips and tricks from how other people go about solving problems.
